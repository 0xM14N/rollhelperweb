RollHELPER is simple, chrome extension (addon) for traders on csgoroll.com, which I originaly created just for myself and couple of my friends, mainly for counting coins (tracking into the spreadsheet) and mass delisting of items.

Later I have added more functionalities, such as pricing of the items in the depo page, autoaccepts of deposits, discord notifications. Also User Interface in form of popup has been added since I noticed there is more people using my addon, so everyone can modify the settings now.

The RollHELPER extension is also avaiable right now on chrome extension store (link at the chrome logo).
